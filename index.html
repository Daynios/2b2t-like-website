4Builders4Tools (4b4t.me)
4b4t is an Anarchy server. This means no rules, no bans/kicks, and no admin interference! In this server you can pretty much do whatever you want, start bases, make new friends, and attack anyone that gets in your way! The IP to connect is 4b4t.me. It has been on the same world since the 22nd of June 2022 and the map will never reset.
